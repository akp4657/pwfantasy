Welcome to Fantasy Graps!

What is Fantasy Graps?

Fantasy Graps is an online game where participants manage a virtual roster made up of real professional wrestlers. Points are earned based on the wrestlers' real-world performance in matches and presence on the show. You compete against other players' fantasy teams throughout a season to win the league!

Fantasy Sports Scoring Breakdown

Each fantasy sport has its own unique scoring system. Below, we break down the typical categories for scoring and how you can earn points:

Match Scoring

  • Win: +2 points for winning a match.
  • Finisher: +0.75 points for each "finisher" used within a match.
  • Interference: +0.5 points for interfering in a match
  • Kickout: +0.25 points for each successful kickout or submission escape within a match.
  • Pin: +0.20 points for each pin attempt within a match.

General Scoring

  • Return: +15 points for a wrestler returning after being away for more than 8 weeks.
  • Turn: +10 points for a wrestler turning.
  • Character Change: +5 points for debuting a new character.
  • Commentary: +3 points for a wrestler being on commentary.
  • Match: +3 points for participating in a match.
  • Crossover: +2 points for a wrestler being in another promotion.
  • Social Media: +1 point per 10,000 interactions on social media during week of show.
  • Segment: +0.25 points per minute of segment wrestler is involved in.

FAQ

How do I make trades or pickups?

Currently, you cannot make trades with other players, but you are able to add/drop wrestlers in the My Team tab.

When are points updated?

Points are updated as the shows are airing. Points are refreshed on the website every five minutes.

What happens if my wrestler gets injured or is unavailable?

If a wrestler is injured or unavailable, then you are able to drop that wrestler and add another at no cost. Just make sure to keep an eye for the return bonus.

Can I change my team name?

Yes! In the My Team tab, just click on the team name to change your team's name whenever you'd like.

How do I win?

The goal is to have your roster score the most points by the end of the season. Points are earned through your roster's performance.

How long is a season?

There are four seasons:

  • January-April | WrestleMania Season
  • April-July | All In Season
  • July-October | SummerSlam Season
  • October-January | World's End Season
